MCA at KMCT College of Engineering, Kozhikode

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program at KMCT College of Engineering, located in Kozhikode, Kerala, is a 2-year full-time postgraduate course aimed at equipping students with advanced knowledge in computer science and software development. The program is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE.
